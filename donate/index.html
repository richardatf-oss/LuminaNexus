Support LuminaNexus

Your gift helps us build a living digital sanctuary — a place of light, learning, and connection rooted in Torah, Kabbalah, and wonder.

At this stage, donations are handled directly by the founder. Please reach out by phone or email to discuss how you’d like to support the work.

Note: If you need a receipt or specific documentation for your records, let us know when you contact us so we can provide what you need.

How Your Support Helps

Your support may help fund:

  • Development of online study tools and sacred learning apps
  • Hosting costs and technical infrastructure for LuminaNexus.org
  • Creation of free resources, guides, and interactive libraries

If you have a specific project or dedication in mind, we’re happy to talk about it.

Ways to Give

You can begin by contacting us directly. We’ll follow up with the most appropriate method (online, mail, or other arrangements) based on your needs and location.
