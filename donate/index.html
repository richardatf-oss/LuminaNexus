LuminaNexus stands downstream from older, deeper roots. We invite you to support those foundations first, and then — if it feels right — to partner with the LuminaNexus Foundation.

Giving order · סֵדֶר הַתְּרוּמָה

When people ask how to give, we answer in this order: Temple Institute → Chabad → LuminaNexus Foundation. The tree should honor its own roots.

1 · The Temple Institute

The Temple Institute works toward the study, memory, and future of the Beit HaMikdash. We encourage donors to support their work before supporting LuminaNexus.

Give to the Temple Institute →
2 · Chabad

Chabad centers bring Torah, mitzvot, and practical kindness into daily life across the world. After supporting the Temple Institute, we invite you to support your local Chabad house or the broader Chabad network.

Give to Chabad →
3 · LuminaNexus Foundation

Once you have supported these foundations, you are warmly invited to support the LuminaNexus Foundation — the living digital sanctuary behind this site, dedicated to slow study, embodied practice, and constructive creation.

Give to LuminaNexus Foundation →
LuminaNexus Foundation · קֶרֶן לוּמִינָהנֶקְסוּס

Status & tax information

LuminaNexus Foundation has applied to the IRS for recognition as a tax-exempt organization under Section 501(c)(3). Our federal tax-exempt status is currently pending.

At this time, we cannot guarantee that donations are tax-deductible. If the IRS approves our application, tax-deductible status for eligible donations may be retroactive to our formation date. Donors should consult their own tax advisors about how this applies in their specific situation.

We are also registered with our state as a nonprofit entity and will comply with applicable charitable solicitation laws.
