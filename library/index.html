How to Use This Library · איך להשתמש בספרייה

Not a Line to Finish, but a Circle to Walk

  • Begin with Foundations. Align language and posture.
  • Move into Daily Practice. Choose one blessing, one phrase, or one small action you can actually carry into this week.
  • Then explore the deeper trunks. When you are ready, walk into Quantum Etz Chaim and the 231 Gates as long-form paths.

Whenever you feel lost, return to the Foundations shelf. The Library is designed to meet you where you are, and to be walked more than once.
