Slow Series · מַסְלוּלִים אָטִיים

Longer journeys, one step at a time.

Slow Series are multi-week chavruta paths on Shabbat, tefillah, middot, and the Seven Laws. Each trunk has a simple arc: a core idea, a few sources, and one concrete practice for each week.

לֹא לְגָמְרֵי סִיוּם, אֶלָּא דֶּרֶךְ לָלֶכֶת בָּהּ — קְצָת מִקְרָאוֹת, קְצָת שִׂיחָה, וּמַעֲשֶׂה קָטָן לְשַׁבּוּעַ.

You can walk a trunk straight through six weeks, or dip into one week when it speaks to where your life is. Each trunk can grow later into printable sheets, app flows, or full courses.
