Text

Tehillim 27:1–3 — Psalms 27:1–3.

Open on Sefaria: Psalms 27:1–3 on Sefaria .

Question

In this psalm, “Hashem is my light and my salvation” becomes the anchor against fear. Which single phrase in these verses feels like an anchor for you today, and why?

Action

Choose one concrete situation today where fear or anxiety usually shows up. Before you enter it, pause for one breath and quietly repeat your chosen phrase from Tehillim 27:1–3.
