About LuminaNexus

LuminaNexus is a living digital sanctuary — a meeting place of Torah, Kabbalah, and constructive creation. We build tools, libraries, and guided experiences that help ancient wisdom feel close, usable, and alive.

What we’re building

LuminaNexus is designed as a calm place for slow study: texts, diagrams, apps, and creative projects that invite real practice — not performance, not noise.

Some strands that gather here:

  • Digital libraries and study tools grounded in Torah and the inner tradition
  • Interactive maps and visualizations (Etz Chaim, letters, gates)
  • Books and series that blend spiritual insight with imaginative storytelling
  • Experiments at the edge of faith, creativity, and modern code

Who is behind it?

LuminaNexus was founded by Richard Lee — a writer and builder exploring the intersections of Torah, mysticism, and modern systems.

The aim is steady: to serve, to illuminate, and to return a small corner of the internet back toward the light.
