Your side of the table

Bring one question. Not all of them. Write it slowly, as if you were speaking to a friend across the table.

Hints: “Explain Shema Yisrael (Devarim 6:4)”, “Kavod vs Anavah”, “Tehillim 27:1 with classic mefarshim”.

Your chavruta will…
  • Read your question as if you were sitting together in a quiet beit midrash.
  • Point you to sources you can open yourself on Sefaria.
  • Respond slowly, trying to clarify rather than to win.
  • Invite you to turn the answer into one small action this week.

For now this is a design prototype; the Ask button does not yet send anything. It’s here to help you shape the question you would bring to a real chavruta.
