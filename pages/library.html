The Library

Offered freely for learning. No urgency. No manipulation. Just clean, accessible resources in a Torah-first spirit.

How This Library Works

  • Small and honest: we add resources carefully, not endlessly.
  • Clear labeling: text vs. commentary vs. reflection.
  • Respectful boundaries: not psak, not conversion guidance.
  • Free offerings: if something is here, it is given without pressure.

Starter Kit

If you’re new, begin here. Keep it simple. One page a day is enough.

  • Torah First — read the covenant page once.
  • The Seven Laws — pick one law per week and practice gently.
  • Study Paths — choose one path and stay with it for a season.
  • Chavruta — use the prompts to study without overwhelm.
